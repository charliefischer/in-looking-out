Megan Thimm

Megan is an illustrator.

"The work is a painting of my local graveyard. It's a really peaceful place and in this time I'm so grateful to have access to spaces like these; I know not everyone has the luxury. I hope that during lockdown there will be a re-realising of the importance of free public spaces."
