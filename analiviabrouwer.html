Analivia Brouwer

Analivia is a photographer and artist.

"I come from a human rights background, but am now working towards doing illustration and fine art photography. I made an illustration based on my daily walk. I live in Amsterdam so my daily walks are not bad - I spend them photographing and staring up at the architecture. At the moment, it is very sunny here which results in everybody hanging out of their windows, usually with some (or a lot of) wine and music. This illustration is my idea of how I would design my own perfect quarantine space to be looking out - full of colour!"
