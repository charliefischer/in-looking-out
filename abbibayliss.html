Abbi Bayliss

Abbi is a Bristol based artist and writer. I specialise in monochrome digital portrait illustrations, aswell as colourful childrens books.

"My motivation behind this piece is to alleviate the pressure of being ultra productive during lockdown. I aim to spread a positive message that it’s okay if we’re simply at home relaxing and recharging, as long as we’re at home. The list is my realistic daily routine, that hopefully everyone can relate to."
