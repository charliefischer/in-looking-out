Jack Seymour

Jack is an architecture student based in Manchester.

"Currently I am studying architecture, in my final year of masters. I have always loved painting from the days of art back in high school, and would try to find commissions as and when I could. This will be the first painting I have done in about a year, and is actually of the first picture I uploaded on Instagram 8 years ago. My art style has derived from studying artists like Edward Hopper, where there is a focus on lighting to create atmosphere. So when I stumbled across this old photo, I thought it was perfect for meeting the brief and captures how we are feeling in these times of quarantine. As a dog lover, during my art studies I was interested in the humanistic qualities of ‘man’s best friend’ and their relationships with us. Dogs represent our playful and free nature, and in this piece are reflection of our wanting to be enjoying the outside. Being unable to source a canvas in these times, this is painted on a stretched pillow case."
