Daniela Sosa

Daniella is a freelance illustrator based in London.

"I recently got a linocut set and did some experiments. Started with some nice fruit and then, inevitably, led to this. As much as I wanted to avoid doing something virus-related, I couldn't come up with anything else, because this is all there is, for now. Linocut seems like the right medium for these times, it's slow and focused, so time really flies by."
